NicheKit implements publication-grade meta-analysis entirely in one place. Upload your Excel sheet, map your variables, and generate RevMan 5–style forest plots for continuous and binary outcomes — plus Network Meta-Analysis and ROB2 / ROBINS-I traffic-light plots. NicheKit, which is a part of Nichev1.0 project, keeps workflows unified from data upload to reporting. No R. No server. No data leaves your device.

Analysis 3 — Network Meta-Analysis
Network Meta-Analysis (NMA) synthesises evidence from studies comparing multiple treatments simultaneously, estimating both direct and indirect effects. Frequentist graph-theoretic approach matching R's netmeta() with DerSimonian-Laird random effects.
Continuous (MD/SMD) — pairwise format

Binary (OR/RR) — arm-level format matches R pairwise()
